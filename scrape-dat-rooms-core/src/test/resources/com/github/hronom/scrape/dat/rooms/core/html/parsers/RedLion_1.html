    100 percent Non-Smoking. This newly constructed 45 room Settle Inn and Suites will offer guests fourteen suites, an indoor pool, complimentary breakfast, along with 32 inch plasma televisions, microwaves and refrigerators in each room, wireless internet and a community room. Each guest room is tastefully appointed with a selection of maple and cherry wooded furniture, granite counter tops in a relaxing tones of greens and golds. A business center is located in the lobby to help you complete your projects. Our Whirlpool room is a great choice for any special occassion. Oversized tub for 2, with additional in suite seating.
