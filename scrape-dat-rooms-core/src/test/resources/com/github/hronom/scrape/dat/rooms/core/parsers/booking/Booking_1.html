Directly across the street from the MGM Grand in Las Vegas, this motel is just steps from several exciting attractions and offers a wide range of convenient amenities.

Motel 6 Las Vegas Tropicana is within walking distance of several casinos including Excalibur and the Luxor. McCarran International Airport is 1.9 km from the property. The Las Vegas Convention Center is less than 5 km away.

Before exploring Las Vegas, guests of Tropicana Motel 6 can enjoy a free cup of morning coffee. In the evening, guests will enjoy this pet-friendly motel's outdoor pool and coin laundry facilities.
