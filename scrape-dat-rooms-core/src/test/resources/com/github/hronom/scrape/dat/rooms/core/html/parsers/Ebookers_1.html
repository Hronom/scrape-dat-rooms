Overview

Welcome to Americas Best Value Inn Adelanto Victorville located on State Highway 395. Our newly renovated hotel offers world class amenities such as 24 hour concierge desk- multilingual staff- and free wireless high-speed Internet access available throughout the building and in all guest rooms- also guests will have access to our business center with computer and fax service. Children 11 years of age and under stay free in parent*s room with existing bedding. For your convenience we offer an onsite guest laundry facility. Start your day our free continental breakfast and lobby coffee service. We have 21 nicely decorated spacious standard and accessible guest rooms that feature all of the comforts of home including a microwave- mini-fridge- coffeemaker- 32 inch flat screen satellite TV- HBO- ESPN- clock radio- hairdryer- iron- ironing board- desk with lamp- and complimentary local call access. Most importantly- we pride ourselves on our great service. Everything we do is driven by our desire to provide exceptional service- dedication to detail and a friendly- comfortable environment. Late check-out is allowed at a charge of 20 USD. Please ask at front desk. We offer pre-schedule shuttle service round trip to Ontario International Airport is available to our guests at a charge of 60 USD. Americas Best Value Inn Adelanto Victorville proudly sits in the heart of San Bernardino County close to shopping- restaurants- and entertainment venues as well as the major business generators of Adelanto. We are only minutes from the High Desert Mavericks Stadium- Racetown 395- Race Town Motorcycle Park- California Route 66 Museum- Pumpkin Massacre Haunted House and Southern California Logistics Airport. At Americas Best Value Inn Adelanto Victorville- We*ve Got You Covered from the time you hit the road until your head hits the pillow.
